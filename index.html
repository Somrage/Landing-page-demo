We are Viktor's Brewery

A young Izhevsk brewery that honors the traditions of brewing that has developed over centuries of beer culture, however, brings a little of it's own, Udmurt, charm, which gives a unique style to our beer.

Golden stars of our beer

"Izhevsk Porter" abv 8.2 og 18

The amazing, dense taste of Baltic-style porter hits the palate with its strength, but leaves behind a slight coffee bitterness on the tongue. Ideal for chocolate dessert

"Udmurt Lager" abv 4.2 og 12

The refreshing summer taste of barley malt is complemented by an unobtrusive aftertaste of Žatec hops. Great to drink very chilled in the heat.

"Blanche Selychka" abv 5.1 og 16

The bright wheaten taste of Belgian-style blanche with pronounced fruity notes will be a great addition to a hearty lunch.

"Kama velvet" abv 4.6 og 13

The special purified water of the Kama River gives a special charm to the classic dark beer.
